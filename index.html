About Me

I am a recent graduate of the Mathematics and Engineering program at Queen’s University, with a specialisation in Systems and Robotics, providing me with the knowledge and capabilities for understanding complex science, mathematics and finance. I have strong mathematics skills and experience with MATLAB, C, VHDL, and assembly language. Additionally, I have completed the educational requirements for PMP and CAMP Certification. I am characterized as an individual with a strong work ethic and who is organized, responsible, and versatile. As you can see, my website is in progress.
